I Have a Dream

Sep 19th, 2012

Martin Luther King, Jr, needs no introduction. I am sure that every one must have heard of him in one context or other. But most of the people have forgotten what he said. I happen to listen to his famous speech “I Have a Dream”. He talked about equality, freedom and justice. But what amazed me the most was that this phrase was not even there in his origional speech. His origional speech was titled “Normalcy, Never Again.”.


But towards the end of the speech black American singer Mahalia Jackson shouted to Dr. King from the crowd, “Tell them about the dream, Martin.”. And he deviated from his origional speech. He started preaching, punctuating his points with “I Have a Dream.”. And this speech went on to become the Top American Speech of the 20th century. This immediately reminded me of his famous saying:
